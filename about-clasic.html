About

BEVERLY GRANT

Hi! I am Beverly Grant
A Ghanaian-based photographer who finds a lot of joy in capturing moments and immortalizing them into memories.
I’ve always had passion for creativity long before i stumbled upon photography and when I did, I discovered it was the right medium to unleash all my creative abilities and ingenuity.
Capturing emotions is one of the most important parts of my work. I feel strongly about making photographs that I can connect with. Even though the emotions of my subjects are the point of interest in my documenting, I find a reflection of myself in them. The goal is to show the beauty, grace, strength and enthusiasm of my subjects by placing them in exaggerated situations to highlight an aspect of who they are.
